About Us

Built on Practical Experience. Driven by Compliance.

Founded in 2021 and officially registered in the State of Oregon, M1 Safety Consulting was created with a clear purpose: to make Environmental, Health & Safety (EHS) practical, defensible, and effective for real-world operations.

Who We Are

M1 Safety Consulting is an independent, Portland-based EHS consulting firm delivering end-to-end Environmental, Health & Safety support to organizations across multiple industries. Our services range from regulatory compliance and audits to training, on-site safety leadership, and full EHS program development.

While we are locally rooted in Oregon, our team supports clients nationwide through a flexible combination of remote consulting and on-site engagement, allowing us to meet EHS needs wherever work is performed.
